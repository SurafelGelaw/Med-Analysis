

Instructions


1. When you are presented with the home screen, select a department from the side navigation menu.
2. When you are in a particular department, you are presented with input fields.
3. Once there, you can manually insert as much as data as you want.
4. And the program will generate a chart instantaneously.
5. once done you can click on the bars to save your chart as a PDF, JPG, and PNG.
6. Or you can click on download chartDataAsCSV button to downlod your data in the form of a CSV file.
7. Alternatively, you may wish to go to the about page to learn more about the program and its Developer.
8. Thank you for using the program.
9. Enjoy.
